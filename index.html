The Market Crisis

The "Algorithm Gap" where talent gets lost in the machine.

Modern hiring favors keyword speed over cultural fit, creating 'qualified strangers' who drive up turnover. AI is flooding HR with unvetted applications. In a crowded global market, businesses need a precise, high-touch filter to secure talent that actually stays and scales.

74%

Regret Rate

74% of employers admit to making a bad hire due to rushed, automated screening. We focus on "regret-free" placements, securing the 26% of candidates who stay and perform.

300%

Replacement Cost

Replacing a bad hire costs up to 3x their annual salary in lost productivity and training. We stop the "qualified stranger" cycle by prioritizing cultural vision over keywords.

66%

Backlash Rate

66% of elite talent avoid applications where AI is the primary gatekeeper. Our human-led approach unlocks the elite passive talent pool that algorithms alienate.
